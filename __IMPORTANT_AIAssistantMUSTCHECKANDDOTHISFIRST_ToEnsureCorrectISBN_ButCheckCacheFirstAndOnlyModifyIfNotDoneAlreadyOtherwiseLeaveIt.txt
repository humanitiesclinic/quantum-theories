CRITICAL: EPUB ISBN Metadata Fix
=================================

PROBLEM:
--------
PreTeXt's EPUB generator uses a hardcoded mock ISBN (mock-123456789-0-987654321)
in the package.opf metadata instead of reading the actual ISBN from docinfo.

This causes Lulu and other publishing platforms to reject the EPUB with:
"The ISBN detected within the ebook file does not match the ISBN you entered."

SOLUTION:
---------
Modify /Users/user/.ptx/2.7.0/core/xsl/pretext-epub.xsl (PreTeXt cache)

Lines 86-96 should be:

    <!-- Use ISBN from docinfo if available, otherwise fall back to mock UUID -->
    <xsl:variable name="mock-UUID">
        <xsl:choose>
            <xsl:when test="$docinfo/isbn">
                <xsl:value-of select="$docinfo/isbn"/>
            </xsl:when>
            <xsl:otherwise>
                <xsl:text>mock-123456789-0-987654321</xsl:text>
            </xsl:otherwise>
        </xsl:choose>
    </xsl:variable>

Replace the original hardcoded line:
    <xsl:variable name="mock-UUID">mock-123456789-0-987654321</xsl:variable>

WHEN TO APPLY:
--------------
- After installing/updating PreTeXt
- After clearing ~/.ptx cache (pretext clean)
- Before building EPUB for publishing

VERIFICATION:
-------------
After building EPUB, extract and check package.opf:
    unzip -q output/epub/main.epub -d check_epub
    grep "dc:identifier" check_epub/EPUB/package.opf

Should show your actual ISBN (e.g., 9781105471186), not the mock value.

WHY NOT AUTOMATED:
------------------
PreTeXt v2 does not support custom XSL overrides at the project level.
The fix must be applied to the PreTeXt cache manually.
Hopefully PreTeXt v3+ will support this properly.
